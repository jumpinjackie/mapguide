Changing Viewer Options
 
 

Use the View Options command to change the units of measurement in which distances are displayed on the status bar and in tool tips.

To change viewer options

  1. Choose the View Options command.

    The Viewer Options task will appear in the Task Pane on the right side of the viewer window.

  2. Under Display Distances In, click a unit of measurement.
  3. Click Save.
