Zooming Dynamically
 
 
 

To zoom dynamically in the DWF Viewer

  1. Choose the Zoom Dynamic command.
  2. On the map, click and drag the cursor to move the map view.
  3. Move the cursor up to zoom in or down to zoom out.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 as necessary.

To zoom dynamically in the AJAX Viewer

  • Move the slider up or down to zoom in or out.
