The MapGuide Viewer Interface
 
 

MapGuide AJAX Viewer

The MapGuide Viewer interface is divided into the following vertical panels:

  • Left panel - This panel is divided into two panes. The Layers Pane is on top. It displays the layers and layer groups in the map. The Properties Pane displays properties of selected items and appears below the Layers Pane. Right-click the list of layers to access commands related to the layer list and the display of map layers. In the DWF Viewer, right-click the Properties Pane additional options.
  • Center panel - This panel displays the map. Right-click the map for access to commands related to viewing the map.
  • Right panel - This panel displays the Task Pane. Depending on your actions, the Task Pane displays task options and information or help topics.
