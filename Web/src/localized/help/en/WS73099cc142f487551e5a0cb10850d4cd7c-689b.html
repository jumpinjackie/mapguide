Understanding MapGuide Viewer
 
 

MapGuide Viewer lets you view maps published by Autodesk MapGuide Enterprise or MapGuide Open Source, and distributed over intranets or the Internet. Using MapGuide Viewer, you can zoom in and out on a map, find specific features, select features that meet certain criteria, display information about selected features, print the current view of the map, and more.
