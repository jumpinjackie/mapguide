MapGuide Open Source - Getting Involved as a User

Join the Mailing Lists

Join the mailing lists to learn what other users are doing with MapGuide Open Source, ask questions, get answers, and when you get comfortable jump in and answer questions. The users@mapguide.osgeo.org mailing list provides a wealth of information for all MapGuide Open Source users from novices to experts.
